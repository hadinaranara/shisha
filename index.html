About us

PT Atlas Charcoal Indonesia is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality charcoal products based in Indonesia. Established with a vision to provide sustainable energy solutions, we specialize in manufacturing premium charcoal made from natural and renewable resources. Our products are meticulously processed to ensure top-tier performance, catering to both domestic and international markets.

With dedication and innovation, PT Atlas Charcoal Indonesia has established a strong presence in the global market, exporting to numerous countries across the Middle East. With capacity to more than 15 forty-feet containers per month, our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and sustainability has made us a preferred partner for businesses seeking premium charcoal products.

We aim to provide clean energy solutions that meet international standards while supporting the environment and empowering local communities. At PT Atlas Charcoal Indonesia, we ignite the future with sustainable energy.
